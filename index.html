About the Book

Atomic Habits by James Clear is a guide to building good habits and breaking bad ones. It focuses on making small, incremental changes that compound over time.

About James Clear

James Clear is a speaker and writer focused on habits, decision making, and continuous improvement. His work has been featured in The New York Times, Time, and The Wall Street Journal.

Micro Habits in Action

Try habits like “2-minute rule”, environment design, and habit stacking to create long-lasting behavior change.
